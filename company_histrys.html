Key Milestones of Bharathbrands

E-Commerce Venture (2017-2019)

1. Founding Bharathbrands (2017): Established Bharathbrands in 2017 with the ambitious goal of competing with global e-commerce giants, laying the foundation for a journey of innovation and growth.

2. E-commerce Platform Launch (2019): By 2019, successfully developed and launched a robust e-commerce platform, strategically targeting businesses in India and creating a foundation for future expansion.

Shift to ERP (2019)

3. Comprehensive ERP Solution (2019): Recognizing the evolving needs of businesses, initiated the development of a Comprehensive ERP Solution in 2019, aiming to provide more than just an e-commerce platform.

Venturing into Healthcare (2018-2020)

4. Healthcare Systems Initiative (2018): Acknowledged the significance of healthcare systems and began development in 2018, marking the company's entry into the healthcare sector.

5. ERP and Hospital Information System (HIS) Integration (2020): Achieved a milestone by integrating ERP with a Hospital Information System (HIS) by 2020, streamlining healthcare solutions and contributing to the improvement of medical services.

Focus on System Integration (2020-2021)

6. API Gateway Development (2020): Emphasized the importance of system integration by developing a robust API gateway in 2020, facilitating seamless connectivity between various systems.

7. Microservices Architecture Realignment (2021): Realigned the architecture to a microservices-based approach in 2021, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in response to the growing demand for integrated solutions.

Service Automation System (2021)

8. Service Automation System Development (2021): Successfully developed a Service Automation System in 2021, orchestrating services and integrating microservices from previous systems, showcasing a commitment to automation and efficiency.

9. Realization of Development Potential (2021): Recognized the potential of service orchestration and API gateway in expediting new developments, setting the stage for continued innovation.

Educational Systems (2021-Present)

10. Incorporating components into Educational Systems (2021-Present) Continued the development journey by incorporating lessons learned into educational systems, ensuring a comprehensive suite of solutions to meet the evolving needs of the education sector.

Machine Learning and AI Focus (From Dec 2022):

11. Focus on Machine Learning and AI (From Dec 2022): Recognized the pivotal role of machine learning and AI decision-making platforms, signaling a strategic shift towards integrating advanced technologies into existing systems to stay at the forefront of technological advancements.

Bharathbrands remains committed to its mission of providing innovative and comprehensive IT solutions, continually adapting to industry needs and pushing the boundaries of technology.
