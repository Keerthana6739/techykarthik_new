Join Our Journey at TechyKarthik.Com

Welcome to TechyKarthik.Com! We invite consultants from around the globe to join our innovative development team. Our structured process ensures seamless collaboration and delivery of high-quality solutions for our clients and partners

Recruitment Process

Application Review:

Consultants submit their applications through our portal.

Online Interviews:

Selected candidates participate in online interviews to assess their skills and compatibility with our team.

Sample Projects:

Consultants work on sample projects to demonstrate their abilities.

Test Projects:

Candidates undertake test projects to showcase their proficiency. Successful completion with a minimum score advances them to the recruitment stage.

Consultant Levels
  • Junior Consultant: After recruitment, consultants start as Junior Consultants.
  • Consultant: Achieved after successfully completing three projects.
  • Senior Consultant: Earned after successfully completing nine projects in total.
Work Agreements

Before joining our team, consultants are required to review and sign the following agreements:

  • Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): Ensures confidentiality of client and proprietary information.
  • Consultant Agreement: Defines the consultant's relationship with TechyKarthik.Com, including terms of engagement and responsibilities.
  • Work Collaboration and Delivery Structure Agreement: Guides collaboration between consultants and TechyKarthik.Com, outlining project management processes, milestones, and deliverables.
Project Assignments

Once onboarded, consultants receive assignments managed by project managers from TechyKarthik.Com. Each assignment includes a Statement of Work specifying project scope and timelines.

Global Collaboration

Virtual Collaboration: Our collaboration tools facilitate seamless teamwork among consultants worldwide.
Experienced Guidance: Consultants receive support from our experienced developers and team leaders, ensuring projects meet high standards and client expectations.

Development Framework

Unique Framework: Our proprietary development framework integrates services and individual assignments efficiently.
Service Orchestration: Leveraging our platform, developments are orchestrated for cohesive end-to-end product creation.

Confidentiality and Quality Assurance

Non-Disclosure Agreements: Consultants adhere to strict NDA guidelines to maintain project confidentiality.
Testing and Validation: Projects undergo rigorous testing by our project teams to ensure quality and reliability.
