Product Overview: REVENUENILE - Comprehensive Revenue and Service Management System


REVENUENILE is an advanced Revenue Management System that seamlessly maps organizational services to charges and fees through a sophisticated transaction coding system. This powerful platform extends beyond revenue collection, offering a complete information system, including Executive Information System (EIS), Management Information System (MIS), and standard reporting functionalities. The software's flexibility is further amplified by its robust API gateway, facilitating integrations with third-party agencies, service providers, and data sources.



Service Mapping and Transaction Codes:

REVENUENILE efficiently maps every organizational service to charges and fees using a systematic transaction code system, ensuring accurate and transparent revenue management.

Payment Gateway Integration:

Seamlessly integrated with a payment gateway, the platform automates payment processing for every service, providing a secure and streamlined payment experience.

Flexible API Gateway:

The software's flexible API gateway allows seamless integration with third-party agencies, enabling collaboration with service providers, data sources, and partners involved in service delivery.



Comprehensive Information System:

REVENUENILE offers a complete Information System, including Executive Information System (EIS), Management Information System (MIS), and standard reporting, empowering users with valuable insights for strategic decision-making.

EIS, MIS, and Reporting:

REVENUENILE caters to all levels of information needs, from Executive Information System (EIS) for strategic leaders to Management Information System (MIS) for operational insights, along with standard reporting for day-to-day analytics.



Service Mapping and Transaction Codes:

Users within the organization can easily define and launch service categories and groups, making services accessible to customers through user-friendly portals.

Third-Party Agency Integration:

Enable integration with agencies delivering services, acting as data sources for customer verifications, or partnering in service delivery. The platform fosters a collaborative ecosystem.

Customer Portals

Launch service categories and groups in all customer portals, providing customers with effortless access to a wide array of services delivered by the organization.
